## How We Work

Creative Labs is a Shopify-focused development studio. We build custom storefronts, integrations, and growth systems for eCommerce brands.

**Our core services:**
- Custom Shopify theme development
- Shopify Plus migrations and upgrades
- Third-party app integrations
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO)
- Ongoing retainer support

## Onboarding Checklist

After signing your contract, complete these steps within the first 48 hours.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Your Project Brief">
    Fill in your project brief via the onboarding form shared in your welcome email. Include:
    - Brand assets (logos, fonts, color palette)
    - Inspiration links or reference stores
    - Current platform details (if migrating)
    - Target launch date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Shopify Access">
    1. Log into your Shopify Admin
    2. Go to **Settings → Users and permissions**
    3. Click **Add staff account**
    4. Enter `dev@thecreativelabs.io`
    5. Grant **Full permissions** (required for theme development)
    6. Click **Send invite**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Project Channel">
    We use **Slack** for day-to-day communication. You'll receive an invite to a shared Slack channel within 24 hours of onboarding. All major decisions and approvals happen here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Kick-off Call">
    Check your calendar for a 45-minute kick-off call invitation. This is where we align on scope, timeline, and milestones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your Project Timeline

| Phase | Duration | What Happens |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Discovery | Days 1–3 | Brief review, architecture planning, asset gathering |
| Design | Days 4–14 | Wireframes, mockups, client review rounds |
| Development | Weeks 2–6 | Theme/feature build, internal QA |
| QA & UAT | Days 2–5 | Client testing, bug fixes, final approvals |
| Launch | Day 1 | DNS cutover, post-launch monitoring |

<Note>
  Timelines vary by project scope. Your PM will share a detailed milestone schedule during the kick-off call.
</Note>

---

## Communication Expectations

- **Response time:** We aim to reply within 4 business hours on weekdays (9am–6pm EST)
- **Feedback rounds:** Most projects include 2–3 rounds of revisions per phase
- **Approvals:** Written approval (Slack message or email) is required before we proceed to the next phase
- **Scope changes:** Any changes outside the agreed brief are scoped separately and require a written change order
