## Project & Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does a typical project take?">
    A standard Shopify theme build takes 4–8 weeks depending on scope. A full migration (platform change + custom theme) typically takes 8–12 weeks. Your project timeline is confirmed during the kick-off call.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What do I need to provide at the start of the project?">
    Brand assets (logos, fonts, color palette), a completed project brief, Shopify staff access, and any existing assets (current theme exports, product photos, copy).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many rounds of revisions are included?">
    Most projects include 2 rounds of revisions per phase (design and development). Additional rounds are billed at our hourly rate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I make changes to the scope after we've started?">
    Yes — scope changes are handled via a change order. We'll scope the change, provide a cost estimate, and proceed once you've approved it in writing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who owns the code after the project is done?">
    You do. All custom code developed for your project is fully owned by you upon final payment.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Shopify

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need Shopify Plus?">
    Not always. Shopify's standard plans work for most businesses. We recommend Plus when you need custom checkout, B2B features, advanced automation (Flows), or multi-store setups. We'll advise during scoping.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit the theme myself after launch?">
    Yes, via the Shopify theme editor (no code required for content changes). For code edits, we recommend using a duplicate of the live theme so you have a backup. For significant changes, reach out — editing production theme code incorrectly can break your store.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a theme I bought on ThemeForest?">
    We can work with third-party themes for modifications, but we don't recommend ThemeForest themes for serious stores — they often have bloated code, poor performance, and limited support. We'd rather build you something clean.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My store was built by someone else. Can you take it over?">
    Yes, we do store takeovers. We'll audit the existing code, document what's there, and provide a plan before committing to any changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payments & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do I get invoiced?">
    Project work is typically billed in milestones: 50% upfront to begin, 25% at design approval, and 25% at launch. Retainer clients are invoiced monthly on the 1st.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    ACH bank transfer, wire transfer, and credit card (3% processing fee applies to card payments).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I miss a payment?">
    Development is paused when invoices are 14 days overdue. We'll always reach out before pausing work.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Support & Maintenance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's included in post-launch support?">
    All projects include 14 days of post-launch bug fixing at no charge (bugs only — not new features). After that, support is available on a retainer or per-ticket basis.

  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug?">
    Submit a ticket at [support.thecreativelabs.io](https://support.thecreativelabs.io). Include a screenshot, steps to reproduce, and the device/browser you're using.

  <Accordion title="How quickly do you respond to support tickets?">
    We aim to respond within 4 business hours for standard tickets. Critical issues (store down, checkout broken) are treated as emergencies — we respond within 1 hour during business hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer ongoing maintenance retainers?">
    Yes. Retainer packages start at 5 hours/month. [Contact us](mailto:hello@thecreativelabs.io) to discuss options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Migrations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can you migrate my store from WooCommerce / BigCommerce / Magento?">
    Yes, we handle platform migrations. What transfers: products, collections, customer accounts, and historical orders. What may require rebuilding: custom features, discount logic, and integrations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I lose SEO rankings when I migrate?">
    Not if we do it correctly. We set up 301 redirects from all old URLs, preserve meta data, and test crawlability before launch. Temporary ranking fluctuations are normal (1–4 weeks) but should recover.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does a migration take?">
    A data migration alone can take 1–2 weeks. A full migration with a new custom theme is typically 8–12 weeks.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
